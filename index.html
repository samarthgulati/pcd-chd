Overview

We will start from the basics - on how to draw simple shapes and gradually move towards drawing organic forms, animation, and interaction in a fun and engaging way. By the end of the workshop you would have acquired skills that you can then apply to:

  • create abstract art masterpieces
  • design info-graphics and custom interactions for websites
  • computationally design patterns for fabrics
  • iterate over design ideas for architectural installations

Also, you will get to take home a framed print out of your creation.

Instructor's Bio
Samarth Gulati

Samarth is currently leading UX Design at StayQrious, a YC W20 edtech startup, as an Independent Product Designer. Previously, he was working at Adobe - San Francisco for 2.5 years as an Experience Developer, where he built web based prototypes and visualizations for their Search and Sensei (AI/ML) team. He comes with a masters in HCI from University of Michigan - Ann Arbor.

Outside of work he builds generative art snippets on CodePen and at times brings them to physical world using a plotter / 3D printer. He is an avid listener of audiobooks, especially in cog-sci, and behavioral psychology genre.
